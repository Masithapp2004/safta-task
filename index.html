Biotechnology solution could be the key to

unlocking Saudi Food security

Launched in July 2024 under the patronage of the Ministry of Environment, Water, and Agriculture (MEWA), the Saudi AgriFood Tech Alliance brings together national and international members, including government bodies, research institutions, academia, private sector companies, and non-profits. The Alliance fosters collaboration to advance agricultural and food technology solutions in Saudi Arabia, driving national-scale deployment of innovative AgriFood technologies.
